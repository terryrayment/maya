## Posting Rules
1. **Hook in first 1-2 seconds** — always
2. **No dead time** — cut ruthlessly
3. **Post at peak times**: 12pm PT and 6-7pm PT weekdays, 10am PT weekends
4. **Reply to every comment** within 1 hour
5. **Pin the best-performing video** to your profile
6. **Duet/stitch** relevant dog content when it appears on FYP
7. **Use 3-5 hashtags** per post (not 30)
8. **Include CTA** in every caption: "link in bio" or "officeofmaya.com"

## Week 3+ Cadence
- 2 posts/day minimum
- 1 new script + 1 remix/variation
- Double down on any video that hits 10K+ views
- Kill formats that get <500 views after 48 hours
- Test 1 trending format per week

## Content Production Notes
- Batch film 5-7 videos in one session
- Keep raw footage library for remixing
- Natural light, clean backgrounds (MAYA aesthetic)
- Phone camera is fine — authenticity > production value
- Use CapCut for editing (free, TikTok-native)
