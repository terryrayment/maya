## 4. HOW THE TRACKING WORKS

1. **Affiliate signs up** at officeofmaya.goaffpro.com/create-account
2. **Auto-approved** — no manual review required
3. Affiliate receives a **unique referral link** in their dashboard
4. We create their **custom coupon code** (MAYA-[NAME]) in Shopify
5. When a customer clicks the referral link OR uses the coupon code, the sale is attributed to the affiliate
6. Commission is tracked in GoAffPro and paid monthly

### Technical Setup
- GoAffPro app installed and connected to Shopify
- Core tracking snippet enabled in theme
- Post-checkout popup: **OFF** (keeping it exclusive, not mass-market)
- Auto-approve: **ON**

## 10. ADMIN — HOW TO MANAGE

### When someone responds "Interested":
1. Send them the signup link: **officeofmaya.goaffpro.com/create-account**
2. They sign up (auto-approved)
3. Go to Shopify Admin > Discounts
4. Rename an available MAYA-FOUNDING code to **MAYA-[THEIRNAME]**
5. DM them: "You're in! Your code is MAYA-[NAME] (10% off for your followers). Your 30% commission tracks automatically through your dashboard. Free product stack shipping this week."
6. Ship them a free Complete Stack

### Monthly payout:
1. Go to GoAffPro > Payouts
2. Review commission earned per affiliate
3. Process payment (PayPal, bank transfer, or manual)

### Checking performance:
- GoAffPro > Dashboard: Total sales, clicks, conversions
- GoAffPro > Affiliates: Individual affiliate performance
- GoAffPro > Sales: Transaction-level detail
