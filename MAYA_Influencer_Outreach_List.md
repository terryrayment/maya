## NOTES ON METHODOLOGY

- All accounts listed were found through web research using multiple influencer databases, brand partnership directories, and platform searches.
- Follower counts are approximate and based on the most recent data available as of early 2026; counts fluctuate.
- Engagement levels are estimated as High (5%+ engagement rate or notably active comments), Medium (2-5% engagement rate), or Low (under 2%).
- Some Tier 2 accounts exceed the 25K target range but were included because their aesthetic, content quality, or breed relevance makes them exceptional fits for MAYA's brand positioning.
- Accounts marked with specific dog names were verified through search results and influencer databases.
- DM drafts are written to feel personal and non-transactional, referencing specific content and dogs by name where possible.

## RECOMMENDED OUTREACH PRIORITY

1. **Highest priority:** Tier 1 accounts with under 15K followers (accounts 1-9) -- these have the most targeted audiences and highest conversion potential
2. **Second priority:** Tier 3 LA-based accounts (accounts 71-85) -- local partnership creates brand credibility and enables in-person content
3. **Third priority:** Tier 4 vet professionals (accounts 86-100) -- professional endorsement validates MAYA's vet-formulated positioning
4. **Fourth priority:** Tier 2 aesthetic accounts (accounts 41-70) -- drives brand awareness and positions MAYA in the premium lifestyle space
